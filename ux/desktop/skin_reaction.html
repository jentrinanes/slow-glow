Clinical Education

Purging vs. Irritation

Distinguishing between an expected accelerated cell turnover cycle and compromised barrier function is critical for maintaining long-term skin health.

Purging (Expected)

  • Occurs in areas of frequent breakouts.
  • Microcomedones surface faster than usual.
  • Typically resolves within 4-6 weeks.
  • Not accompanied by widespread stinging.

Irritation (Barrier Damage)

  • Occurs in new areas, often with redness.
  • Accompanied by stinging when applying bland moisturizers.
  • Skin feels tight, shiny, or excessively dry.
  • Requires immediate cessation of actives.
